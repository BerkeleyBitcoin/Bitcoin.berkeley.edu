Meeting with Olaf Carlson-Wee, Coinbase Employee #1

Monday April 28, 2014 6:30-8:30PM @ UC Berkeley, Dwinelle Hall, Room 106

Join us for a conversation with Olaf Carlson-Wee, the first employee at Coinbase.

Olaf has been actively involved in the bitcoin community for three years. He completed his undergraduate thesis on trustless cryptographic networks, focusing on the implications of the mass adoption of bitcoin. He is an expert in decentralized ledger protocols, cryptography-based financial instruments, and businesses involved in the bitcoin space. He joined Coinbase a year ago as the first employee.
